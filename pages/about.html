Our Legacy

INDUSQA is a leading consultancy and certification service provider with over 30 years of industry experience. We specialize in helping service organizations—mainly IT Service and Education—achieve and maintain international standards.

Our core competencies include promoting process-based work culture and emphasizing the importance of metrics and information security. We believe that quality and security are not just certifications, but the foundation of sustainable business growth.
